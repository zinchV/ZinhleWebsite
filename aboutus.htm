Who we are

CompCo is a platform that educates about issues pertaining to computers and mobile devices.Our aim is to make sure that users of computers and mobile devices are always up to date with information that has to do with their computers or mobile devices including information about any upgrades that they may need to conduct in their devices and how to protect their devices.This is done through providing material in the form of articles,websites and videos.Links are provided to the different material.
